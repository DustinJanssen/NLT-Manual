Open School

Open School events are held on Saturdays with the aim of recruiting new students to Lilyvale. Your participation and responsibilities during these events may vary.

On the Friday before Open School, you may be asked to assist with preparations, such as setting up signs for the toilets. Consult with Fujinami-sensei to determine which signs should be used.

During the Open School event itself, if you do not have classes scheduled, you will likely be asked to help care for younger children while their parents attend a lecture. This typically takes place in the study room during 3rd and 4th periods. Your role will be to play and interact with the children, ensuring they have a positive experience while their parents learn more about the school.

By engaging in these Open School events, you contribute to showcasing the welcoming and nurturing environment that Lilyvale has to offer prospective families.
