Cleaning

Cleaning is an essential part of the daily routine at Lilyvale Elementary School. It takes place after lunch and before the fifth period, from 13:10 to 13:25. As an NLT, your role is to guide and oversee the students while they clean the area you are assigned to.

Cleaning Groups

Students are divided into two cleaning groups: A.C.E. and B.D.F. These groups will rotate between cleaning their classroom and another designated location. The rotation generally occurs every fortnight.

Big Clean

Occasionally, the school will conduct a "big clean" where cleaning time and the fifth period are combined. Non-classroom cleaning will take place from 13:45 until the end of the fifth period. During these big cleans, your role remains the same: guide and oversee the students as they clean the assigned areas.

Remember, cleaning is an opportunity to teach responsibility and teamwork to the students, so make sure to support and encourage them throughout the process.
